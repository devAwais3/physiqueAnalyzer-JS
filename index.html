BMI Calculator

Formula: BMI = weight (kg) / height (m)²

Health Insights

Understanding BMI :

BMI (Body Mass Index) is a numerical value of your weight in relation to your height. It's a reliable indicator of body fatness for most people.

BMI Categories:

  • Underweight: Less than 18.5
  • Normal weight: 18.5 to 24.9
  • Overweight: 25 to 29.9
  • Obese: 30 or greater

Maintaining a healthy BMI reduces your risk of heart disease, diabetes, and certain cancers. Regular monitoring helps in maintaining optimal health.

Height Calculator

Formula: Height = √(weight (kg) / BMI)

Health Insights

Height and Health :

Height is influenced by various factors including genetics (accounts for about 80% of variation), nutrition, and overall health during growth years.

Medical conditions that can affect height include growth hormone deficiency, Turner syndrome, and certain genetic disorders. Early detection and treatment are crucial.

Height is an important factor in health assessment as it helps determine healthy weight ranges and can indicate potential health risks or conditions.

Weight Calculator

Formula: Weight = BMI × height (m)²

Health Insights

Weight Management :

Body weight is a key indicator of overall health. Being underweight can lead to decreased immune function and nutritional deficiencies, while excess weight increases risk of chronic diseases.

Weight affects metabolism, joint health, cardiovascular function, and energy levels. Small changes in weight can have significant impacts on overall health.

Maintaining a healthy weight through balanced nutrition and regular physical activity is crucial for long-term health and disease prevention.
